- **Jean-Luc Godard**: 1960s (peak), 1970s (political), 1980s (late), 1990s-2000s (final)
- **Satyajit Ray**: 1950s-1990s (entire career)
- **Edward Yang**: 1980s-2000s (complete filmography)
- **Wong Kar-wai**: 1990s-2000s (peak auteur period)
- **Martin Scorsese**: 1970s (New Hollywood), 1980s (genre-auteur)
- **David Lynch**: 1980s-2000s (postmodern cult-auteur)
- **Stanley Kubrick**: 1960s-1990s (formalist auteur)
- **Federico Fellini**: 1960s-1970s (late modernism)
- **Francis Ford Coppola**: 1970s (New Hollywood peak)

### Core Density by Decade
- **1960s**: Highest director count (modernist rupture = most generative wave)
- **1970s**: Second highest (political cinema expansion)
- **1980s**: Moderate (postmodern/genre turn)
- **1990s**: High (global arthouse + American indie convergence)
- **2000s-2010s**: Selective (late auteurs + festival cinema)
